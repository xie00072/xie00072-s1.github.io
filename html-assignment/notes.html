HTML tags List:

<!doctype HTML>
The first thing we add to our HTML document which defines what is the type of this document.
<nav></nav>
The navigation of the page, this will contain the links to other pages of the website.
<main></main>
Primary content of the page.
<article></article>
The tag used for containing independent piece of content. It could be shared as stand alone piece of content and will still make sense.
<aside></aside>
Secondary content on the page, this information is not part of the main topic of the page and is not required.
